Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide free estimates for all services. After reviewing your request, we'll provide a detailed quote before any work begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of work unless other arrangements are made in advance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely! We carry full liability insurance and all necessary licenses to protect you and your property.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons. However, we can often accommodate urgent requests.

Do you offer emergency services?

For urgent repairs, please call us directly at (555) 123-4567. We'll do our best to accommodate emergency situations.

What if I need to reschedule?

We understand plans change. Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule your appointment.
